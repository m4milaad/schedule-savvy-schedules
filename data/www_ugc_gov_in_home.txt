About
University Grants Commission
The University Grants Commission (UGC) came into existence on 28th December,
1953 and became a
statutory Organization of the Government of India by an Act of Parliament in 1956, for the
coordination,determination and maintenance of standards of teaching, examination and research in
university education.

Message from
Chairperson UGC
The University Grants Commission (UGC) was established by an Act of Parliament in 1956 to coordinate, determine, and maintain standards of teaching, examination, and research in university education. Now, UGC has become an important pillar of higher education.
